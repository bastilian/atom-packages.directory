Line Breaker

Line-Breaker Package

An Atom package that breaks long lines of text.

Keybindings: * alt-cmd-enter breaks the selected lines or the current line.

Breaks long lines on the preferred line length boundaries while keeping the indentation of the first line in place.

Works well with contiguous selections of text or long single lines of text and with Atom-supported comments:
